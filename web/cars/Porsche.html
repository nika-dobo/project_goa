Porsche

**Porsche** is a German luxury and sports car manufacturer known for its high-performance vehicles, engineering excellence, and iconic designs. Founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche, the company originally provided vehicle development and consulting services. Porsche's most famous model, the **911**, debuted in 1964 and remains a symbol of the brand's heritage and innovation. Other popular models include the **Cayenne** (SUV), **Macan** (compact SUV), **Panamera** (luxury sedan), and **Taycan** (electric sports car). Headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany, Porsche is part of the Volkswagen Group and continues to push boundaries in automotive technology, particularly in electrification and motorsports. Its vehicles are celebrated for their blend of performance, luxury, and practicality.

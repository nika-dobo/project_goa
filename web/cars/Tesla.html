Tesla

Tesla, Inc. is an American electric vehicle (EV) and clean energy company founded in 2003 by engineers Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning, with Elon Musk joining soon after as its largest investor. Tesla is best known for its electric cars, such as the Model S, Model 3, Model X, and Model Y, which have revolutionized the automotive industry by popularizing electric vehicles with long ranges, innovative technology, and high performance. In addition to vehicles, Tesla produces solar energy products (solar panels, solar roofs) and energy storage solutions (Powerwall, Powerpack). Tesla's mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy.

**Mercedes-Benz** is a renowned German luxury automobile manufacturer, established in 1926. It is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany, and is part of the Mercedes-Benz Group. The brand is globally recognized for its premium vehicles, high-performance engineering, and innovative technology. Mercedes-Benz produces a wide range of vehicles, including luxury sedans, SUVs, sports cars, electric vehicles (EVs), and commercial vehicles. Popular models include the C-Class, E-Class, S-Class, G-Class, and the EQS from its electric lineup. The company is also a pioneer in automotive safety and technology, introducing features like airbags, anti-lock braking systems (ABS), and advanced driver-assistance systems. Its slogan, **"The Best or Nothing,"** reflects its commitment to quality, innovation, and luxury.

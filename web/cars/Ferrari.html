Ferrari

**Ferrari** is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer known for its high-performance vehicles, innovative design, and strong association with motorsport. Founded in 1939 by **Enzo Ferrari**, the company is headquartered in Maranello, Italy. Ferrari is renowned for its distinctive prancing horse logo and its participation in Formula 1, where it holds the record for the most Constructors' Championships. Ferrari produces iconic models like the **Ferrari 488**, **Ferrari F8 Tributo**, and **Ferrari Purosangue** (its first SUV). These cars are celebrated for their powerful engines, cutting-edge technology, and unparalleled craftsmanship, making Ferrari a symbol of speed, luxury, and exclusivity.

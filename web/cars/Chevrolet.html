Chevrolet

Chevrolet, often referred to as Chevy, is an American automobile brand established in 1911 by Louis Chevrolet and William C. Durant. It is a division of General Motors (GM) and is known for producing a wide range of vehicles, including cars, trucks, and SUVs. Chevrolet is recognized for models like the Chevrolet Silverado (pickup truck), Chevrolet Malibu (sedan), and Chevrolet Equinox (SUV). The brand is known for its focus on performance, reliability, and innovation, and has been a significant player in both the American and global automotive markets.

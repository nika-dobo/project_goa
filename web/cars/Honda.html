Honda

Honda is a Japanese multinational conglomerate known for manufacturing automobiles, motorcycles, and power equipment. Founded in 1948 by Soichiro Honda and Takeo Fujisawa, the company quickly became a global leader in the automotive and motorcycle industries. Honda is renowned for producing reliable, fuel-efficient vehicles and innovative technologies, such as hybrid and electric vehicles. It also holds a strong presence in motorsports, including Formula 1 and MotoGP. With a commitment to sustainability, Honda is actively investing in environmentally friendly technologies, including hydrogen fuel cell and electric vehicles.

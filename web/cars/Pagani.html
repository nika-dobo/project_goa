Pagani

**Pagani Automobili S.p.A.** is an Italian manufacturer of high-performance hypercars, founded in 1992 by Horacio Pagani. The company is based in San Cesario sul Panaro, near Modena, Italy, a region renowned for luxury automotive engineering. Pagani specializes in producing bespoke vehicles that combine cutting-edge technology with artistic design. Its most iconic models include the **Pagani Zonda** and the **Pagani Huayra**, both revered for their exquisite craftsmanship, aerodynamic innovation, and use of lightweight materials like carbon fiber. Pagani cars are highly exclusive, often custom-made for each client, and command multimillion-dollar price tags, making them a symbol of automotive luxury and engineering excellence.

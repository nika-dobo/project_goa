BMW

**BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG)** is a German multinational corporation known for producing luxury vehicles and motorcycles. Founded in 1916 and headquartered in Munich, Germany, BMW initially manufactured aircraft engines before transitioning to motorcycles and automobiles. The brand is synonymous with premium quality, innovative technology, and performance-focused design. BMW's product lineup includes sedans, SUVs, sports cars, and electric vehicles under its core brand, as well as MINI and Rolls-Royce. It is recognized for its iconic kidney grille design, cutting-edge engineering, and the slogan "The Ultimate Driving Machine." BMW also invests heavily in sustainable mobility, with its BMW i series focusing on electric and hybrid models.

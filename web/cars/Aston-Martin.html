Aston Martin

**Aston Martin** is a British luxury car manufacturer known for its elegant design, high performance, and association with sophistication. Founded in 1913 by Lionel Martin and Robert Bamford, the company gained fame for producing iconic sports cars and grand tourers. Aston Martin vehicles are often linked to the James Bond film series, starting with the DB5 in *Goldfinger* (1964). The brand combines craftsmanship, cutting-edge technology, and timeless design. Its lineup includes models like the DB series, Vantage, and the Valkyrie hypercar. Aston Martin also explores electrification, aiming to balance heritage with innovation. The company is headquartered in Gaydon, Warwickshire, England.

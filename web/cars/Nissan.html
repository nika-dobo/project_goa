Nissan

Nissan is a Japanese multinational automobile manufacturer headquartered in Yokohama, Japan. Founded in 1933, it is known for producing a wide range of vehicles, including sedans, trucks, SUVs, and electric cars. Nissan is recognized for its innovation in automotive technology, including advancements in electric vehicles with the Nissan Leaf, one of the best-selling electric cars globally. The company operates in over 160 countries and is part of the Renault-Nissan-Mitsubishi Alliance, a strategic partnership with Renault and Mitsubishi Motors.

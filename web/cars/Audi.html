Audi

Audi is a German luxury automobile manufacturer headquartered in Ingolstadt, Bavaria. It is part of the Volkswagen Group and is known for producing high-performance, stylish, and technologically advanced vehicles. Audi's lineup includes sedans, SUVs, coupes, convertibles, and sports cars, with an emphasis on innovative design, quattro all-wheel drive, and cutting-edge technology such as virtual cockpit displays. The brand's slogan is "Vorsprung durch Technik," meaning "Advancement through Technology," reflecting its focus on innovation and high engineering standards. Audi has a strong presence in motorsports, particularly in endurance racing.

Bugatti

**Bugatti** is a renowned French luxury automobile manufacturer, founded in 1909 by Italian-born industrial designer **Ettore Bugatti**. Known for its blend of artistic design and cutting-edge engineering, Bugatti gained fame for producing high-performance sports cars, including the **Bugatti Type 35** in the 1920s, one of the most successful racing cars of all time. The brand was revived in the late 20th century and is now part of the **Volkswagen Group**. Modern Bugatti cars, such as the **Veyron**, **Chiron**, and **Bolide**, are celebrated for their groundbreaking speed, luxury, and advanced technology. Bugatti vehicles are widely regarded as symbols of opulence and engineering excellence, with top speeds exceeding 400 km/h and exclusive production runs.

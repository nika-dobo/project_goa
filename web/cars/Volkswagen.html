Volkswagen

Volkswagen is a German automaker founded in 1937 and headquartered in Wolfsburg, Germany. It is one of the largest car manufacturers in the world, producing a wide range of vehicles, including compact cars, sedans, SUVs, and electric vehicles. Volkswagen's most iconic model is the Volkswagen Beetle, first introduced in the 1930s. The company operates globally and owns several other brands, such as Audi, Porsche, Bentley, and Lamborghini. Volkswagen has made significant strides in electric mobility with the introduction of models like the ID.4 and ID. Buzz as part of its strategy to transition toward sustainable transportation.
